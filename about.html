Jinghe Cai

Works Works About About

Jinghe Cai is a product designer with a multidisciplinary background in design thinking, affective computing, and visual storytelling. Her experience spans cutting-edge HCI research, start-ups, AI systems, and iOS app design.

With over five years of research experience, Jinghe has published in leading HCI venues and served as a reviewer for ACM CHI and ACM Multimedia between 2022 and 2024. She sees research not just as a tool for understanding, but as a foundation for spotting actionable design opportunities. Beyond digital products, she is also a cartoonist. Between 2020 and 2025, she published comics that reflect the tension, awkwardness, and warmth in everyday life.
