Jinghe Cai

Works Works About About

Jinghe Cai is a multidisciplinary product designer based in the U.S., with a background that bridges design thinking, affective computing, and visual storytelling.

With over five years of research experience, Jinghe has published in top HCI venues and served as a reviewer for ACM CHI and ACM Multimedia between 2022 and 2024. She values research not just as a tool for understanding, but as a foundation for identifying actionable design opportunities.

Beyond her design work, Jinghe is also a visual artist and comic creator. Between 2020 and 2025, she published a series of comics exploring the subtleties of human emotion that reflect on emotion and everyday interactions.
